I am working as an Assistant Professor (Grade-II) at the [Department of Computer Science](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/goa/ComputerScienceInformationsSystems/ComputerScienceandInformationSystems), [BITS Pilani Goa Campus](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/goa/index.aspx). I am also a Doctoral Researcher in Machine Learning at the same department. I am working with Senior Professor [Ashwin Srinivasan](https://www.bits-pilani.ac.in/goa/ashwin/profile).

My research is focused on the areas of Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Neuro-Symbolic Models, and Optimisation. My doctoral research focuses on the study of stochastic optimisation to search very large discrete spaces. An application of this study is in the area of Inductive Logic Programming ([ILP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inductive_logic_programming)), which is a subfield of symbolic artificial intelligence. Further, the application of the study extends to neuro-symbolic machine learning, a type of which is a combination of ILP and Deep Neural Networks. 

In 2014, I have received M.Tech from [VSSUT (Formerly UCE), Burla](http://www.vssut.ac.in/), where I worked on the area of pattern recognition using evolutionary algorithm trained neural networks. I graduated with B.Tech degree from [NIST Berhampur](http://www.nist.edu) in the year 2012. I am a **Silver Medalist** in both M.Tech and B.Tech for my academic performance. 

I have worked as an Assistant Professor at [NIST Berhampur](http://www.nist.edu) for over a year during 2014 - 2015. I have also worked as IASc-INSA-NASI Summer Research Fellow at [ISI Kolkata](http://www.isical.ac.in/). I have qualified national level competitive examinations such as [GATE](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graduate_Aptitude_Test_in_Engineering) (thrice) and [UGC-NET](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Eligibility_Test).
